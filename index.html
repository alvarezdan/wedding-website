OUR STORY

WE ARE GETTING MARRIED ♥

Once upon a time, in the south eastern realm of the northern American lands, lived this very unique woman. This woman was like something out of a fairy tale - long golden flowy hair, heart stopping eyes and a big beautiful smile. A very pleasant sight indeed.

Along came a young lad, who had been traveling the world for five years prior. On his journey he encountered this fabulous woman. The site of her was like nothing he had ever seen before. The feeling he felt was like nothing he had felt before. He knew, he must get to know this woman.

It didn’t take long before the two became close companions, and not long after, sweethearts. This was the beginning of a new era for both, the two very different lifestyles quickly became one epic journey. Together, for the past three and a half years, it has been nothing but lively adventures traveling the world and genuinely enjoying each other’s company, along with their pup Maddi, every single day.

This story has only just begun…
